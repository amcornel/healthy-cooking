Greek Joghurt with Blueberries

  • Ingredients:
  • 500 gr. Greek Joghurt
  • 250 gr Blueberries
  • Honey
  • Chopped Almonds (optional)
  • Cocoa Nibs (optional)
  • Serves 4

How To Prepare
Mix the Greek Joghurt with the Honey and divide it into four bowls.
Add the Blueberries on top of the Joghurt.
Sprinkle the Almonds and Cocoa Nibs on it.
ENJOY!
